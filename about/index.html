About

Startup speed. Corporate scale.

Playground Ventures works at the intersection of startup speed and corporate scale. Our team has spent years inside both new-to-world CPG brands and large corporate venture studios — which means we know exactly where good ideas get stuck, and how to get them moving again.

We've supported early-stage brands finding their first customers, and helped innovation teams inside large food & beverage organizations build the muscle to move like founders — through consumer insights, commercialization, digital experimentation, and hands-on operating support.

The name isn't an accident. We think the best innovation work happens when people are given real room to play — to test, to fail cheaply, and to find what actually works before betting big on it.
